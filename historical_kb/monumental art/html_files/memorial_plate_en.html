Memorial plate - a monumental sculpture dedicated to important historical or other events, significant dates or famous persons, outstanding figures in the form of a plate (block), which installed in areas associated with important historical events, significant dates, life and work famous persons or famous figures, contains font compositions with short biographical information about the famous personality or information about an important historical date (event), may include a relief or pictorial image, usually has area from 1 to 10 sq.m and horizontal arrangement.
